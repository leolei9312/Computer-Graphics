User Instructions

1. Click mouse on right half to see three rotating cone.

2. Click mouse on left half to see crown.

3. Drag your mouse to rotate the UFO!!!!!

4. Use Up, Down, Right and Left to control UFO moving and size of UFO will be changed.

5. Spin button is for increasing or reducing the speed of rotation.

6. speed button is for increasing or reducing the moving speed.

7. Mouse click effects will disappear if you drag mouse.

8. Resize the window and there will be some stars on the sky.
