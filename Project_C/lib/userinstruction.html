User Instructions

1. Press up arrow, down arrow, left arrow and right arrow to move towards different way.

2. Press 'w', 's', 'd' and 'a' to lift up, move down, look right and look left.

3. Press 'u' and 'i' to look up and look down.

4. Press '0' to open/close the front light. Press '9' to open/close the head light.

5. Press '-' and '=' button to modify the ambient.

6. Click the button above to change the mode.
