3. Press 'u' and 'i' to look up and look down.

5. Press 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 to adjust asymmetric camera for Extra Credit 1.

6. Press 'f' to make the camera fly for Extra Credit 3.

7. When dragging mouse, the moving jointed rectangular will move for Extra Credit 4(Using Quaternion).
